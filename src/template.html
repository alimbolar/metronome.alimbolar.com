Metronome is a device that makes a regular, repeated sound to show a musician how fast a piece of music should be played. It helps a musician make music.

Creating music or writing code are very similar. It may eventually end up in the creation of a song or a web app. And we all need a metronome. Whether it's for your personal or business website, let's play some music.. or rather create some code.
